About the Institution

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1985, Priyadarshini Indira Mahavidyalaya is one of the premier fully aided colleges in Odisha, dedicated to delivering quality higher education and empowering students through academic excellence. Located in Junagarh, Kalahandi district, the institution has played a vital role in shaping the educational future of rural and urban students alike.

The college began its journey in the academic session 1985–86 with the introduction of the +2 Arts Stream, offering major subjects such as History, Political Science, Economics, Education, Geography, and Odia. Due to its growing reputation and commitment to student success, the institution expanded in 1989 by launching the +3 Arts Stream with optional subjects including History, Political Science, Economics, Odia, and Education.

To strengthen advanced learning opportunities, Honours courses in Political Science and Economics were later introduced in the undergraduate wing, helping students build strong academic and career foundations.

Continuing its mission of educational development, the college introduced the +2 Science Stream during the academic year 2004–05 with core science subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, and Zoology.

Today, Priyadarshini Indira Mahavidyalaya stands as a trusted institution for higher education in Odisha, known for its experienced faculty, student-focused learning environment, and commitment to academic growth, discipline, and holistic development.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.A. / B.Sc. / B.Com.: Passed Class 12 (10+2) from a recognized board with minimum 45% aggregate marks.

  • M.A.: Bachelor's degree in the relevant subject with minimum 50% marks from a recognized university.

  • BCA: Class 12 with Mathematics as a compulsory subject and minimum 50% aggregate.

  • B.P.Ed.: Bachelor's degree in any discipline + participation in school/college level sports.

  • SC/ST relaxation: 5% relaxation in minimum eligibility marks as per government norms.

  • Age limit: No upper age limit for regular programs. Distance learning available for working professionals.
